Trafalgar — Age of Sail · Sound effects
=======================================

cannon.ogg / cannon.mp3   — broadside / chase volley fire
impact.ogg / impact.mp3   — cannonball landing / hull hit
splash.ogg / splash.mp3   — near-miss water splash

Sources / licenses
------------------
cannon  — USER-SUPPLIED recording (from cannon_fire_01.ogg), provided by the
          project owner for use in this game. Re-encoded to mono OGG/MP3.
impact  — USER-SUPPLIED recording (from cannonball_land0.wav), provided by the
          project owner. Trimmed (4.3s → ~1.25s, long tail removed), normalized,
          re-encoded to mono OGG/MP3.
splash  — SYNTHESIZED from scratch with FFmpeg (filtered white-noise plume) —
          original work, effectively public domain (CC0), no attribution.

The user-supplied cannon/impact were provided for this project's use. The splash
is our own generated placeholder. None require attribution and all are safe to
redistribute inside the .webapp.zip. Any clip can be swapped later by dropping a
replacement at the same filename in web/public/audio/ (no code change).

Processed 2026-06-05 with ffmpeg 7.1.1 — mono, 22.05 kHz, OGG/Vorbis + MP3
(silence-trimmed + loudnorm where applicable).
