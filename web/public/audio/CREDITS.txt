Source / license
----------------
All clips were SYNTHESIZED from scratch with FFmpeg (filtered noise + tone
envelopes) specifically for this project. They are original work created here,
so they are effectively public domain (CC0) — no third-party rights, no
attribution required, and freely redistributable inside the .webapp.zip bundle.

This was chosen deliberately: per docs/sfx-sourcing.md, downloadable CC0 packs
either require authenticated downloads (Freesound) or had an unconfirmed license
badge (OpenGameArt "Battle at sea"), so to guarantee a clean, attribution-free
license we generated our own placeholders. They can be swapped for confirmed-CC0
recordings later without any code change (same filenames in web/public/audio/).

Generated 2026-06-05 with ffmpeg 7.1.1 — mono, 22.05 kHz, OGG/Vorbis + MP3.
